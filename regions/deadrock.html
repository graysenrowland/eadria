Deadrock

Deadrock is the perilous overland ascent between Delmara and Eadria. Delmarans know it as The Waste; Eadrians know it as Deadrock.

The Waste

The few who have gone far at all into The Waste describe it as a terrifying mixture of horrible creatures and shadows of former grandeur, suddenly abandoned by the presence of some great evil.
