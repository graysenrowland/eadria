Draemyr

An inhabited Alari settlement near the edge of civilization.

Near the Deadrock

Draemyr is seen throughout Delmara as an outlying outpost of civilization and is the closest city to the Deadrock.

The lands beyond Draemyr are surrounded by fear, legend, myth, and campfire stories. To many in Delmara, Draemyr stands nearest to a kind of hell, beyond which are tales of feral, uncivilized cannibals and other horrors.

The city is inhabited by those who do not fear living near that edge.
