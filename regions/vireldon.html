Realm of Vireldon

A political realm within Delmara, seated at Axont.

Places

Axont is the capital and seat of the Realm of Vireldon.

The Realm of Vireldon includes Axont, Illadel, Klad, and Icena. Rule over Klad and Icena is unrecognized by their inhabitants.

Celestial Observation

The humans of Delmara, especially within the Realm of Vireldon, have for centuries sought truth by celestial observation. Traditionally, the planetary bodies are correlated to the Veridra, or truth-keepers, as follows:
