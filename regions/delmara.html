Delmara

Delmara is the Land Above, one of the three main regions of Eadria.

The Land Above

Delmara stands above the lands below, separated from Eadria by towering cliffs that disappear into the clouds.

Aurumveldor

Before the Great Deluge, the land above was known by various names. In historical context, that earlier land is now referred to as Aurumveldor.

Deadrock

The treacherous Deadrock serves as a harsh natural barrier between Delmara and the lands below. Though it can technically be traversed by a mountain ascent from Eadria, the way is rife with fearsome creatures and grim legends, and few dare attempt the crossing.

Cities & Places

Alarian Concord: Kel Alari, Bol Boldihr, and Gravenhold. Rule over Gravenhold is unrecognized by its inhabitants.

Realm of Vireldon: Axont, Illadel, Klad, and Icena. Rule over Klad and Icena is unrecognized by their inhabitants.

Unclaimed: Grugrazzir.

Historical: Dughfaldur.

Ondris Sea

Once the Ondris River, the Ondris Sea took the place of Aurumveldor during the Great Deluge.

Dughfaldur

Once a thriving city of Aurumveldor, Dughfaldur now lies entombed beneath the Ondris Sea, its grand halls and bustling streets drowned during the Great Deluge.

The ruins serve as both a tomb and an enigmatic treasure trove, holding the secrets of a bygone age, untouched and shrouded in mystery.

The Hidden Descent

Though few in the lands below realize it, Delmara is connected to Aurnoore through the vast and perilous Underdeep.
