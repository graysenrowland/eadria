Delmara

Delmara is the Land Above, one of the three main regions of Eadria.

The Land Above

Delmara stands above the lands below, separated from Eadria by towering cliffs that disappear into the clouds.

Aurumveldor

Before the Great Deluge, the land above was known by various names. In historical context, that earlier land is now referred to as Aurumveldor.

The Hidden Descent

Though few in the lands below realize it, Delmara is connected to Aurnoore through the vast and perilous Underdeep.
