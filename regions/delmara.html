Delmara

Delmara is the land above, one of the three primary regions of the world.

Deadrock

The treacherous Deadrock serves as a harsh natural barrier between Delmara and Eadria. Delmarans regard the area with horror.

Realm of Vireldon

A human-led realm centered on Axont, Vireldon claims authority across much of eastern Delmara, though that authority is unevenly recognized beyond its settled heartlands.

Vireldon survived the Great Deluge, but with heavy loss of land and people.

  • Axont: seat of power within Vireldon.
  • Illadel: a settlement under Vireldon’s authority.
  • Klad*: a fortified settlement north of Lake Imryll.
  • Icena*: the Icena people and their lands by Lake Imryll.

Alarian Concord

  • Kel Alari: a settlement of the Alarian Concord.
  • Bol Boldihr: a settlement of the Alarian Concord.
  • Gravenhold*: rule over Gravenhold is unrecognized by its inhabitants.

Unclaimed Places

  • Grugrazzir: an unclaimed place in Delmara.

Waters and Ruins

  • Ondris Sea: once the Ondris River, the Ondris Sea took the place of Aurumveldor during the Great Deluge.
  • Dughfaldur: once a thriving city of Aurumveldor, now entombed beneath the Ondris Sea.

* Does not recognize regional sovereignty.
