Ondris Sea

Once the Ondris River, the freshwater Ondris Sea took the place of Aurumveldor during the Great Deluge.

After the Deluge

The Ondris Sea marks one of the great transformations of Delmara. Where the Ondris River once ran, freshwater now covers what had been part of Aurumveldor.

Dughfaldur

Beneath the Ondris Sea lie the drowned remains of Dughfaldur, a city of Aurumveldor lost during the Great Deluge.
