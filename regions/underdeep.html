Underdeep

The Underdeep is the vast, mysterious subterranean expanse that connects Delmara and Eadria.

The Hidden Depths

Beneath Eadria lies the labyrinthine Underdeep, an enigmatic expanse of tunnels, caverns, and underground cities.

Its depths are fraught with danger, from natural hazards to underdwelling creatures, and some speak of the malevolent pantheons of the Dreadara.

A Hidden Bridge

Few realize that the Underdeep forms a hidden bridge between Delmara and Aurnoore, a secret guarded by its perilous nature and by the underdwellers who call it home.
