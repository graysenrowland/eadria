Underdeep

The Underdeep is the vast, mysterious subterranean expanse beneath Delmara.

The Hidden Depths

Beneath Delmara lies the labyrinthine Underdeep, an enigmatic expanse of tunnels, caverns, and underground cities.

Its depths are fraught with danger, from natural hazards to underdwelling creatures, and some speak of the malevolent pantheons of the Dreadara.

A Hidden Bridge

The Underdeep stretches far enough to form a hidden bridge between Delmara and Aurnoore, but the way is divided by an unoccupied, treacherous underdark wasteland.

Delmarans and Eadrians remain unaware of one another.
