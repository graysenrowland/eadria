Eadria

Eadria is the lower southern region, separated from Delmara by the cliffs and horrors of Deadrock.

Regions

Eadrians know their own lands, including the Lands of Aurnoore, the Isles of Argent, and the Endless Desert.

They are unaware of Delmara above.

Places

Known places marked on the map include Kinallen, Faybourne, Fallbay, Aurnoore, Vereduhr, Orma’aiqua, Rivercrest, and Kar-Tarihr.

Eadrian Traditions

Oldir is Eadrian, not Delmaran.

The Old Way is followed by sages known as Eldiri, or Oldiri as they are called by the Elves.
