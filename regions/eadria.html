Eadria

Eadria is the lower southern region, separated from Delmara by the cliffs and horrors of Deadrock. Eadrians are unaware of Delmara above.

Lands of Aurnoore

  • Aurnoore: the long-ruling High Elven capital built into and upon the cliffs.
  • Vereduhr: a dwarven-led bridge-city and fortified gate at Eadria’s primary access point to the Underdeep.
  • Orma’aiqua: a sprawling city of docks, taverns, and fisheries where the river meets the sea.
  • Riverrest: a quaint village of unusually small human-like folk near Orma’aiqua.

Isles of Argent

  • Kinallen: a named place in Eadria.
  • Faybourne: a named place in Eadria.
  • Fallbay: a named place in Eadria.

Underdeep Ruins
