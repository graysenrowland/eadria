Eadria

The Lands Below, known to its inhabitants simply as Eadria.

Regions

Eadria is used both as the common name of the world as a whole and, more narrowly, for the Lands Below.

Known regions include the Lands of Aurnoore, the Isles of Argent, and the Endless Desert.

Places

Known places marked on the map include Kinallen, Faybourne, Fallbay, Aurnoore, Vereduhr, Orma’Aiqua, Rivercrest, Eldiri, and Kar-Tarihr.
