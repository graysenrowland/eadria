Lake Imryll

Lake Imryll is a serene expanse of crystal-clear waters, revered by the Icena as a sacred and magical place.

The Living Waters

The lake provides food and sustenance to the Icena and is regarded as a sacred, life-giving presence.

The surrounding waters reflect the untouched beauty of the Icena woodlands, deepening the tranquility and reverence that define Lake Imryll’s place in Icena culture.

The Sacred Island

At the heart of Lake Imryll lies a small and intensely sacred island, shrouded in mystery and accessible only for specific rituals and ceremonies conducted by the Yavethra.

The island is a focal point of the Icena’s spiritual connection to the lake and is believed to house ancient forces and ethereal beings.

The Hidden Depths

The elves believe magical beings and unseen forces move within the lake beyond mortal sight.
