Aurnoore

Aurnoore is the long-ruling capital of the Lands of Aurnoore, built into and upon the cliffs.

Ancient Authority

Aurnoore has been in power so long that hardly any remember a time before its rule.

The city has few true enemies. The Underdeep is considered protected by Vereduhr, while the Endless Desert stands between Aurnoore and whatever lies beyond it.

The Court

Aurnoore is governed by a court and council. With few immediate military threats, the court busies itself with matters of coin, diplomacy, academics, and inventions.

Its inventions are magical, mechanical, and scholarly alike.

The Cliff City

Much of Aurnoore is built into and upon the cliff, giving the city the illusion of a dreamlike city suspended in the air.

The sight is breathtaking and powerful in an airy, almost heavenly way.

Knowledge and Eldiri

Many sages and scholars dwell in Aurnoore because of the knowledge gathered there.

Its library is sought by many from far and wide, including several notable Eldiri.
