Cairn Aurumthariel

The active keep and hold of the Keepers of Volelia, built before the Great Deluge.

The Keeper Hold

Cairn Aurumthariel is the keep and hold of the Keepers of Volelia.

It was built before the Great Deluge and remains active in the current age.

It is remembered as an amazing place, shaped by generations of Keepers who have tended, guarded, repaired, and beautified it through years of devotion.
