Sylmora

A contested city near the halfway mark between Axont and Kel Alari.

The Cursed Halfway City

Sylmora is cursed by its location almost at the halfway mark between Axont and Kel Alari.

Across centuries of conflict between the two peoples, Sylmora has seen the horrors of repeated war and ceasefire. At times it has belonged to the Alari; at times to the people of Axont. At times it has fallen into ruin, and in more peaceful years it has been rebuilt as a trading halfway-point.

Yet again and again, its position makes it a strategic military point on the map, bringing the horrors of war upon her people.

Volelian Interventions

Many a Volelian knight has rescued many a mother from Sylmora's suffering. On a few occasions, Volelian knights have even claimed the city in third-party defiance of both armies, forcing a halt long enough to allow evacuation.
