Kel Alari

The capital of the Alari.

Name

The name means “Of the Alari” and is the shortened name used by Axont, since the Alari name is too long and confusing to outsiders.

The Delmaran map was made by Jabril of Axont, and therefore uses Axontian naming conventions for Alari places.
