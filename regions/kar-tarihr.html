Kar-Tarihr

Kar-Tarihr is a ruin deep within the Underdeep, known as the furthest point reached by explorers and adventurers.

The Furthest Known Point

Kar-Tarihr lies deep within the Underdeep, at the far eastern end toward the cliffs of the sea.

Very few explorers and adventurers have ever travelled so far.

The Palace Above the Sea

The original palace of Kar-Tarihr stands high above the sea, with patios overlooking the grand view beyond the cliffs.
