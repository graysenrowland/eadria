Kar-Tarihr

Kar-Tarihr is an abandoned ruin deep within the Underdeep, known only to a few as the furthest point reached by explorers and adventurers.

The Furthest Known Point

Kar-Tarihr lies deep within the Underdeep, at the far eastern end where the city meets the cliff wall above the sea.

Very few explorers and adventurers have ever travelled so far. Few Eadrians know of Kar-Tarihr at all, though more Underdeeplings know its name.

The Ancient City

Kar-Tarihr was once a great city built by unknown ancients.

Now abandoned, it is hardly spoken of and known only by few.

The Palace Above the Sea

The original palace of Kar-Tarihr stands high above the sea. Its patios and verandas overlook the grand view beyond the cliffs, once part of the city’s appeal.
