Alarian Concord

A political realm within Delmara and the realm of the Alari.

Before the Deluge

The Alari pre-date the Great Deluge.

Capital

Kel Alari is the capital of the Alari. The name means “Of the Alari” and is the shortened name used by Axont, since the Alari name is long and difficult for outsiders.

The Delmaran map was made by Jabril of Axont, and therefore uses Axontian naming conventions for Alari places.

Inhabited Places

The inhabited Alari settlements and places shown on the Delmaran map include Kel Alari, Draemyr, Bol Boldihr, and Gravenhold.

Rule over Gravenhold is unrecognized by its inhabitants.
