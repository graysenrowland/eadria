Region

Icena Lake

A mountain lake region associated with the Icena people.

Visual inspiration note: this lake is inspired by Spirit Island on Maligne Lake in Jasper National Park.

Overview

The lake country is reserved for public canon about the Icena homeland, waterside settlements, sacred geography, travel routes, and surrounding mountain terrain.
