About this Archive

This archive preserves what is commonly known, recorded, rumored, or believed within the world.

Known and Remembered

Not every account in the archive should be read as complete or final truth. Some entries reflect regional knowledge, old books, tavern tales, local tradition, scholarly disagreement, or frightened reports from uncertain roads.

Rumor and Record

Where histories differ, the archive may preserve uncertainty rather than resolve it. In Eadria, what is remembered, what is believed, and what truly happened are not always the same thing.
