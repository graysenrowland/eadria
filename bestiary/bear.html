Bear

Bears are large and dangerous beasts found across the wild places of Eadria.

Temperament

Though often solitary, bears are greatly feared by hunters, travelers, and isolated settlements. Their strength and sudden aggression make even a lone beast a serious danger.

Habitats

Bears are most commonly associated with forests, mountain valleys, remote rivers, and the edges of colder lands.
