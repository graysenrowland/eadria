Bestiary

Creatures, beasts, and fearful things remembered in tale, report, and uncertain record.

Land

Creatures of field, forest, waste, mountain, road, and ruin.

Sea

Creatures of rivers, lakes, marshes, coasts, and the great waters such as the Ondris.

Creatures of sky, cliff, canopy, high wind, and open flight.

Underdeep

Creatures of cavern, tunnel, buried water, and the deep places beneath the Terra.

Note: “Fiend” is a broad word in tavern tales and frightened reports. It may describe a monstrous being, a feral creature, a thing glimpsed in darkness, or simply something terrible that no witness could properly name.
