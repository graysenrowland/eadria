Humans

An accepted race of Eadria, though their origins remain strangely alien to many scholars.

Alien Origins

Although humans are an accepted fact in the world of Eadria, scholars note their strangely alien origins.

In all known cases of human civilization, humans seem to have either integrated into, formed alongside, or invaded another people group.

Other races, particularly elves and dwarves, often bear a sense of having “always been there” in many places where humans do not.
