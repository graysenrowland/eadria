Orcs

A naturally large race of Eadria, often misunderstood by neighboring peoples.

Appearance and Reputation

Eadrian orcs are not mindless monsters. They are naturally big, often appearing barbaric to outsiders, and some are quite tall.

Their features are strong and formidable, but they are not defined by exaggerated fangs or brutish caricature.

Outsiders

Many orcs have little use for outsiders, and do not greatly trouble themselves over how other peoples imagine them.
