Wonders and Signs

Most people live their entire lives without seeing anything truly wondrous. Tales of wonders and signs are common in song and story, though clear proof is rare.

Underdeep Beliefs

Eadra and Dreadara are core concepts among Underdeeplings, though their influence and interpretation reach far beyond the Underdeep.

Eadra: beings associated with what Underdeeplings call the Abyss. To surface dwellers, this realm is simply the sky. Many throughout Eadria and Delmara regard the Eadra as benevolent, protective, or sacred, though this is debated and sometimes rejected.

Dreadara: feared beings associated with the deep places of the Underdeep. Whether understood as powers, ancient terrors, or something else entirely, they are rarely spoken of lightly.

The Veridra and Elder Traditions

The peoples of Eadria and Delmara have long sought truth through celestial observation, ancient teachings, oral tradition, and the study of the old powers remembered across the world.
