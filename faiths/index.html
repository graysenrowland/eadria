Faiths & Beliefs

Pantheons, faiths, and religious traditions remembered in the archive.

Delmaran Beliefs

The humans of Delmara have long sought truth through celestial observation, traditionally correlating the planetary bodies to the Veridra, or truth-keepers.

Jaos: Harvest and Goodwill.

Mahlos: Prowess and Courage.

Mevmir: Trade and Skill.

Savis: Justice and Forbearance.

Volelia: Beauty and Life.

Zilneas: Sea and Weather.

Eadrian Beliefs

Oldir: Wisdom and Time.

Underdeep Beliefs

Eadra and Dreadara are core concepts among Underdeeplings. Both Eadrians and Delmarans have some understanding of them through contact with Underdeeplings.

Eadra: beings associated with what Underdeeplings call the Abyss. To surface dwellers, this realm is simply the sky. Underdeeplings have a sense of distrust of the Eadra, while Eadrians and Delmarans often intuitively regard them as good, benevolent, kind, or protective, though this is debated and sometimes rejected.

Dreadara: feared, malevolent pantheons associated with the depths of the Underdeep. Underdeeplings fear the Dreadara, while Eadrians and Delmarans regard them with horror.
