Eadra

Eadra are beings associated with what Underdeeplings call the Abyss. To surface dwellers, this realm is simply the sky.

Underdeeplings have a sense of distrust of the Eadra, while Eadrians and Delmarans often intuitively regard them as good, benevolent, kind, or protective, though this is debated and sometimes rejected.
