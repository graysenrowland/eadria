Dreadara

The Dreadara are scarcely understood. In most places they survive only as dread-filled rumor, half-remembered legend, or a name spoken uneasily in connection with the deepest places of the Underdeep.

Some Underdeeplings fear even acknowledging them. Eadrians and Delmarans who have heard the name rarely agree on what, if anything, it truly means.
