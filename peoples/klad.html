Klad

A human people living north of Lake Imryll in the fort settlement also known as Klad.

First Contact

The Klad are relatively new to the Icena woodlands. Initial contact between the two peoples was wary and tense.

Reimond

The human leader Reimond gradually established peaceful trade and cooperation with the Icena, insisting his people treat them fairly.

The Name Given

The humans possessed no collective name for themselves. The Icena named them Klad after the heated stone ore used in their armor and weapons.
