Peoples

The peoples, cultures, and kindreds remembered in the archive. A people may be associated with a realm, city, settlement, or region without being confined to it.

  • Delmaran Peoples
    • Vireldans — people of the Realm of Vireldon. Associated places: Axont, Illadell.
    • Icena — the Icena people and their lands by Lake Imryll.
    • Klad — a people associated with the fortified settlement north of Lake Imryll.
    • Alari — a people associated with the Alarian Concord.
  • Eadrian Peoples
    • Aurnooreans — people associated with Aurnoore and the Lands of Aurnoore.
    • People of Vereduhr — a dwarven-led people associated with the bridge-city and fortified gate to the Underdeep.
    • Sea-elves of Orma’aiqua — a people associated with the city of docks, taverns, and fisheries where the river meets the sea.
    • People of Riverrest — unusually small human-like folk near Orma’aiqua.
    • Peoples of the Isles of Argent — peoples associated with Kinallen, Faybourne, and Fallbay.
  • Underdeep Peoples
    • Underdeeplings — underdwellers who call the Underdeep home.
