Icena

The Icena are a slight-built people of Delmara who have lived in solitude beside Lake Imryll for centuries.

The Surviving Tribe

Once a larger people, the Icena were nearly wiped out a few generations ago following a surge in the Darkboar population from the eastern forest. As a result, they now remain a relatively small population.

Icena Village

Icena Village is the only Icena settlement.

Near the region shown on the Icena map are unexplored ancient ruins.

The Dead Forest

The Dead Forest is known to the Icena and is a matter of concern to them, though they do not yet dream of the full extent or implications of the crisis.

The Yavethra

The Icena are led by their matriarch, the Yavethra. Upon the Yavethra’s death, a new matriarch is discovered through the Yavalis, the Blessing of Nature’s Chosen Matriarch, in which any living adult Icena woman may be chosen.

Lake Imryll

The village of Icena stands beside Lake Imryll, revered as a life-giving source of food and sustenance. The Icena believe magical beings and unseen forces move within its depths beyond mortal sight.

The Fellhunt

Every century, the youngest warriors of the tribe undertake the Fellhunt, a ceremonial hunt in which the alpha Darkboar is tracked and slain.
