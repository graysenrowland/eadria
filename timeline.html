Timeline

A beginning chronology of Delmara, from the remembered age of Aurumveldor to the present Post-Deluge age.

Aurumveldian Era

The age of Aurumveldor, before the Great Deluge remade the Land Above.

Keepers Established

The Keepers of Volelia are founded in the elder age of Delmara.

The Great Deluge

The dividing catastrophe between Aurumveldor and the Delmara known in the present age. The Ondris River becomes the Ondris Sea, and Dughfaldur is drowned beneath its waters.

Post-Deluge

The current age of Delmara, the Land Above after the flood and remaking of the old world.

Axont Established

Axont rises in the Post-Deluge age.

Klad Settled

The Klad establish their settlement north of Lake Imryll.
