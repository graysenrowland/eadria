# Eadria

## Overview
- Public wiki source for the Eadria setting.
- Public-facing canon belongs in the site pages; campaign material remains non-canonical unless explicitly promoted.
- Use grounded people, culture, polity, geography, and way-of-life descriptions in public prose rather than fantasy ancestry labels.

## Regions

### Delmara
- **Vireldon**: realm centered on Axont.
- **Illadell**: a settlement under Vireldon’s authority.
- **Icena**: a secluded people and their lands by Lake Imryll.
- **Klad**: a fortified settlement north of Lake Imryll.
- **Alarian Concord**: associated with the Alari.
- **Deadrock / The Waste**: a terrifying region between Delmara and the Endless Desert.

### Eadria
- **Aurnoore**: the long-ruling cliff capital of the Lands of Aurnoore.
- **Orma’aiqua**: a sprawling city of docks, taverns, and fisheries where the river meets the sea.
- **Vereduhr**: a fortified bridge-city and gate at Eadria’s primary access point to the Underdeep.
- **Riverrest**: a quaint village of unusually small human-like folk near Orma’aiqua.
- **Isles of Argent**: associated with Kinallen, Faybourne, and Fallbay.

### Underdeep
- **Underdeeplings**: underdwellers who call the Underdeep home.
- **Kar-Tarihr**: an abandoned ruin deep within the Underdeep.

## Workflow Notes
- The live GitHub Pages site is generated into `_site` by `scripts/autolink.js`; do not edit `_site` directly.
- Add public canon entities with pages to `/data/glossary.json` so deployment can autolink them.
- Avoid the term “magic” in public prose unless explicitly approved; prefer subtle wording using wonders and signs.
- Do not use explicit fantasy ancestry labels in public prose unless explicitly approved.
