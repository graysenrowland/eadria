## Overview
- Homebrew setting where multiple TRPG groups contribute across different ages; events may become history/legend for others.
- Peoples: Elves, Tieflings, Orcs (Skyrim-like), Humans.
- Humans seek truth via celestial observation of the **Veridra** (truth-keepers).

## Canon Rules
- World-building is ongoing; **not a “current campaign.”**
- Sessions set in Eadria are **non-canonical** until a **post-session review**.
- Memory priority: Eadria first; other topics trimmed before Eadria.

## Regions

### Delmara (Land Above)
- **Axont**: seat of human power.
- **Illadel**: under Axont; Governor **Percival**.
- **Icena Village** by **Lake Imryll** (Icena wood elves).
- **Klad Settlement** (fortified humans; leader **Reimond**; trade with Icena).
- **Draemyr** (Kel Alari outpost near Deadrock).

### Eadria (Land Below)
- Known as **Eadria** by its people.
- **Aurnoore**: prestigious Elven region/city on cliffside; ally to river peoples.
- **Orma’Aiqua**: elves with strong aquatic ancestry.
- **Vereduhr**: dwarven city-fortress; gateway to Underdark.
- **Ruspe**: first Tiefling city from Vereduhr into the Underdark.

## Rivers & Geography
- **River Imryll**: Axont → Olaf → Icena Village → **Lake Imryll**.
- **Sinheim River**: origin **Lake Ondris** (near Illadel), forks near Klad; one leg to **Lake Imryll**.
- **Deadrock**: treacherous region; rumored cannibals/savages; (undiscovered) land passage link with below.

## Pantheon (Veridra)
- **Jaos** (Harvest & Goodwill)
- **Mahlos** (Prowess & Courage)
- **Mevmir** (Trade & Skill) – linked with Axont’s Trade Council
- **Savis** (Justice & Forbearance)
- **Volelia** (Beauty & Life)
- **Zilneas** (Sea & Weather)
- **Oldir/Oldiri** (Wisdom & Time) – acknowledged both above & below
- Note: **Aurnoore** (below) recognizes only **Oldiri**; **Delmara** recognizes the rest.

## Factions & Politics
- **Trade Council** (Axont): commerce/negotiation/logistics; associated with **Mevmir**.
- **Silver Circle**: sages advising Edric.
- **Warrior Faction**: defense & martial strategy.
- **Raven-associated faction**: includes **Elessa** (Percival’s daughter); name TBD.

## Notable Settlements
- **Axont** (capital), **Illadel** (Percival), **Klad** (Reimond), **Icena Village** (Lake Imryll), **Draemyr** (Kel Alari), **Aurnoore** (cliff city), **Orma’Aiqua**, **Vereduhr**, **Ruspe**.

## Characters
- **Edric**: human leader of Axont.
- **Percival**: Governor of Illadel; father of **Elessa**.
- **Elessa**: in Raven-associated faction (name TBD).
- **Reimond**: Klad leader; allied with Icena.
- **Inistranni**: female Icena leader.
- **Niil & Cenrid**: Icena envoys.
- **Ralstin**: carnival game master; **Korbul**: assistant.

## Traditions & Events
- **Fellhunt** (Icena): youngest warriors hunt the Darkboar alpha every century.
- Carnival near Illadel; tavern run by **Dianni** (outside walls).

## Underdark & Gateways
- **Vereduhr** as strategic gateway; bridge across river; cliff tunnels/overlooks.
- Underdark Tieflings fear **Dreadara** pantheons (humans above call them this).
