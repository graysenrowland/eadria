Geography

The world is divided into 3 primary regions: Delmara, Eadria, and the vast, mysterious Underdeep that connects them.

Delmara

Before the Great Deluge, Delmara was known by various names. In historical context, that earlier land is now referred to as Aurumveldor.

Eadria

Eadria lies to the south. To the north, it is bordered by towering cliffs that disappear into the clouds.

The northern territory of Eadria, at the base of the cliffs, is known as the Lands of Aurnoore.

The Underdeep

Beneath Delmara lies the labyrinthine Underdeep, an enigmatic expanse of tunnels, caverns, and underground cities.

It is a vast and largely unexplored network connecting Delmara and Eadria. Its depths are fraught with danger, from natural hazards to underdwelling creatures, and with old stories in which the name Dreadara remains little more than rumor.

Few realize that the Underdeep forms a hidden bridge between Delmara and Aurnoore, a secret guarded by its perilous nature and by the underdwellers who call it home.

Directional Compass

The peoples of Eadria use a six-point directional compass: Vol, Dread, Cairn, Mere, Wold, and Fen.
