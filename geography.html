Geography

Eadria is divided into three main regions: Delmara, the Land Above; Eadria, the Lands Below; and the vast, mysterious Underworld that connects them.

Delmara

The Land Above.

Delmara is one of the three main regions of Eadria.

Eadria

The Lands Below.

Eadria is one of the three main regions of the world, lying below Delmara and connected to it through the Underworld.

The Underworld

A vast and mysterious realm connecting Delmara and Eadria.
