Geography

Eadria is divided into three main regions: Delmara, the Land Above; Eadria, the Lands Below; and the vast, mysterious Underdeep that connects them.

Delmara

The Land Above.

Before the Great Deluge, the land above was known by various names. In historical context, that earlier land is now referred to as Aurumveldor.

Eadria

The Lands Below.

The land below, lying to the south, is known to its inhabitants simply as Eadria. To the north, it is bordered by towering cliffs that disappear into the clouds.

The northern territory of Eadria, at the base of the cliffs, is known as the Lands of Aurnoore.

The Underdeep

Beneath Eadria lies the labyrinthine Underdeep, an enigmatic expanse of tunnels, caverns, and underground cities.

It is a vast and largely unexplored network connecting Delmara and the lands below. Its depths are fraught with danger, from natural hazards to underdwelling creatures, and some speak of the malevolent pantheons of the Dreadara.

Few realize that the Underdeep forms a hidden bridge between the Heavens and Aurnoore, a secret guarded by its perilous nature and by the underdwellers who call it home.
