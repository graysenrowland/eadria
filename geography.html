Geography

Eadria is divided into three main regions: Delmara, the Land Above; Eadria, the Lands Below; and the vast, mysterious Underdeep that connects them.

Delmara

The Land Above.

Before the Great Deluge, the land above was known by various names. In historical context, that earlier land is now referred to as Aurumveldor.

Eadria

The Lands Below.

Eadria is one of the three main regions of the world, lying below Delmara and connected to it through the Underdeep.

The Underdeep

A vast and mysterious realm connecting Delmara and Eadria.
